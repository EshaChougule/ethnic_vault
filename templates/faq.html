Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that renting outfits online can bring a few questions. Here are some common queries to help you enjoy a smooth and elegant rental experience with Ethnic Vault.

How does Ethnic Vault work?

Users can browse our premium ethnic collections, choose their preferred outfit, select rental dates, place the booking, wear the outfit for their special occasion, and return it after usage.

Do you provide doorstep delivery?

Yes, doorstep delivery services are available in selected areas. Delivery availability depends on your location and order timing.

What happens if the outfit gets damaged?

Minor wear and tear are manageable. However, severe damages, stains, or loss of accessories may result in additional penalty charges.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, cancellations are possible before dispatch confirmation. Once the outfit has been dispatched, cancellation or refund may not be available.

How do I return the rented outfit?

Users can return the outfit on the specified return date. Pickup arrangements may also be provided depending on the location.

Still Have Questions?

Our Ethnic Vault support team is always ready to assist you. Feel free to contact us for booking support, delivery details, sizing help, or rental guidance.
